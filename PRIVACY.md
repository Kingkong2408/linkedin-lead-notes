# Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Lead Notes

Effective date: 2026-05-17

LinkedIn Lead Notes is a browser extension that lets users save private notes and simple lead statuses while browsing LinkedIn profiles.

## Data stored by the extension

The extension stores:

- Lead name
- LinkedIn profile URL
- User-created note
- Lead status
- Last updated timestamp

## How data is stored

The extension stores this data locally in the user's browser using browser extension storage.

## Data transmission

LinkedIn Lead Notes does not transmit user notes, profile URLs, statuses, or saved lead data to external servers.

## Accounts

The extension does not require an account.

## Analytics and tracking

The extension does not use analytics, advertising trackers, or third-party tracking scripts.

## Data sharing

The extension does not sell, share, or transfer user data to third parties.

## User control

Users can delete saved data by clicking the Clear All button inside the extension popup or by removing the extension from their browser.

## Affiliation

LinkedIn Lead Notes is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by LinkedIn.
